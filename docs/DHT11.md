# DHT11 - Temperature & Humidity Sensor

## Overview

#### Measurement range:

##### Temperature

Range [0, 50] °C\
Accuracy ±2 °C

##### Humidity

Range [20, 90] %RH\
Accuracy ±5 %RH\
RH (Relative Humidity): how much humidity is present in the air, compared to how much there could be

#### Sampling Period:

1 second

#### Stabilisation Time

5 min to 10 min

## Pinout

DATA VCC GND

VCC: 3.3V or 5V

## Library

<b>DHT Sensor Library</b> by Adafruit\
